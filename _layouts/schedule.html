As of 04/08/19, this is the remaining schedule for the Call of Duty Black Ops 4 season.



Call of Duty World League February 4th - July 5th This time period is a regular season between the 16 best teams in the game. They compete in multiple matches throughout this time period usually 2 weeks at a time. There are two stages of playoffs that take place later in the season. The teams all meet and compete at a venue in Colombus, Ohio.
